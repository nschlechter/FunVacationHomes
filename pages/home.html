Welcome to Fun Vacation Homes!

Welcome to Fun Vacation Homes, providing both a relaxing trip to the beach or an adventure in Central Oregon. All of these houses are owner managed in order to provide the most personal vacation experience for your family or group.

Black Butte Ranch

Come enjoy the wonderful adventures in Central Oregon. From swimming, tennis, and a sand volleyball court, to golfing, bike riding, and hiking -- Black Butte Ranch has it all. This 4500 square foot home is located on the 2nd hole of the Big Meadow Golf Course. It has a cozy, lodge-like feel equipped with 5 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, a game room, and two large open concept living rooms great for entertaining friends and family!

Sunriver

Sunriver Resort is known for its close proximity to Mt. Bachelor and the Deschutes River, as well as a seemingly endless supply of resort activities. The village mall has many great restaurants and trinket shops to enjoy and is a 7 minute walk from the house. This 5 bedroom, 4 bath home is 4000 square feet and is located on the 13th hole of the Meadows Golf Course. The spacious decks and great room allow for a fun family discussion of the day's activities, whether it is swimming, golfing, bike riding, skiing, tennis, et cetera.

Rockaway Beach

The view of the beach and the Pacific Ocean from this home is absolutely breathtaking. This truly oceanfront home has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. After your long day of sand castles, kite flying, and beach volleyball, enjoy a nice cocktail while watching the sunset from the deck or, even better, the hot tub!
